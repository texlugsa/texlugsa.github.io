# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its tenth virtual Community Meeting (CM) of 2024 on 20 October 2024. 15 Group Members attended our virtual CM via Skype from their homes. Attendance is still strong at our meetings online; however, Members are interested in getting back together in person, a work in progress. Topics discussed were from the LAN: NBRR Museum hosted Fall Train Show kicks off this Friday afternoon with setup and display across the weekend, and future displays at our local LEGO® Resellers. Briefly discussed the invitation to attend Steve J's hosted Holiday Party early in December 2024. Our favorite part of the virtual Community Meeting is the sharing of the latest creations and builds as we go "Around the LUG" near the end of the Community Meeting.

## Attendance

AFOLs: 15

## What We Enjoyed Most

Members enjoyed getting together and seeing everyone, even though it is virtual, since not everyone can do all the events or activities to see each other in person. The regular Community Meetings let Members share or inquire about upcoming events or share recent finds about LEGO® products at local stores in the immediate area. Members also enjoyed sharing their latest creations and their personal LEGO® themed interests, as we went "Around the LUG!"
