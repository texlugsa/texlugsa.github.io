# Event Summary

## Location

Bricks and Minifigs - San Antonio NE, 21850 Bulverde Road, Ste 102, San Antonio, TX 78259

## Summary

On various visits to Bricks and Minifigs - Bulverde after the placement of the Star Wars Display, Members heard incoming customers making various compliments about the LUG's display, they especially liked how the addition of simple wood boards across the top of the display case allowed for the suspending of the Star Wars airships engaged in the battle from above. Several families really liked the display and how elaborate the layout looked re-enacting a Star Wars battle scene, if only in the imaginations of the creators. Members appreciated hearing that customers really liked that the LUG takes the time to create displays for the Store.
