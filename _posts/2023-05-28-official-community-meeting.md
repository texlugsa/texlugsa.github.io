# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its fifth virtual Community Meeting of 2023 on 28 May 2023. 16 Group Members participated virtually via Skype from their homes. The Group discussed a variety of topics: wrapped up our second display of the year - Classic Space and setup third display of year - Castle / Pirates at Krystle's Brick Shop, additionally, wrapped up the awesome displays at the SAPL hosted Pop Madness™ Convention, confirmed charity donation MOC for Brick Rodeo 2023 from the Group as created, built and donated by Robert C, discussed the potential participation of Members at the Big Texas Comicon Convention from 6-8 October 2023 and discussed other LAN topics that might be of interest to the Group, mainly for this Meeting, nothing new to report. Everyone enjoyed hearing about and seeing Members latest creations and builds.

## Attendance

AFOLs 16

## What We Enjoyed Most

Our monthly virtual Community Meeting continues to fill the void of seeing each other personally and keeping the Group connected. Virtually allows Members to meet from the comfort of their homes and still accomplish subjects of interest to the Group. One good thing is that Members enjoy the chance to propose, plan and participate in quarterly TexLUG-SA Social gathering, to show off builds physically, do parts draft of small to medium sets and conduct builds with prizes. Additionally, each month, the Ambassador relays pertinent information from the LAN and gives Members an opportunity to discuss and comment if they desire. Members are encouraging each other to attend and actively participate at the virtual Community Meeting since we continue to put physical Community Meetings on hold, participation was light this month. Members continue to show the rest of the Group their LEGO® builds via these online Skype virtual Community Meetings.
