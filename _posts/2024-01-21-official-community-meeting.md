# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its first virtual Community Meeting of 2024 on 21 January 2024. 16 Group Members and 1 Guest participated virtually via Skype from their homes. Topics discussed were the Winter Display wrap-up and tear down plus short discussion of new MOC to be displayed at Krystle’s Brick Shop, discussed the success of the Holiday Party and the grand time everyone had getting together at the end of the year in person, short discussion on the ongoing efforts to enhance the SAMSAT Area 21™ Cyber City made out of LEGO® bricks and accessories, mentioned that early All Access memberships to Brick Rodeo 2024 were ending very soon. Attendance was grand at the original LBR Store in San Antonio located at North Star Mall. Additionally, we discussed a few events planned for later this year. Our favorite part of the virtual Community Meeting is the sharing of the latest creations and builds as we go "Around the LUG"!

## Attendance

AFOLs: 17

## What We Enjoyed Most

This monthly virtual Community Meeting achieves one of our goals to keep in touch and plan events and activities soon and stay connected. Since we have not agreed to start in person meetings at the mall like before the Worldwide Pandemic, this meeting allows Members to actively participate from the comfort of their homes. Virtual meetings paid off this month as we continue to have a Member of TBRR join our Group discussions and discuss mutual areas of interest for the year. During the “Around the LUG” portion, Members showed off their LEGO® builds and creations, sometimes it is just that they plan to build a recently acquired set and some folks shared progress on their large MOCs they are preparing for Brick Rodeo 2024 this year.
