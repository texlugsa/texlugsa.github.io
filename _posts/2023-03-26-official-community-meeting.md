# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its third virtual Community Meeting of 2023 on 26 March 2023. 15 Group Members participated virtually via Skype from their homes or on the road. The Group discussed a variety of topics: wrap up of the first Social of the year, a great turn out at the local Store, Leads for two Events during the month wrapped up the activities and/or displays and gave other Members a chance to relay their experiences, discussed next month's Spring Train Show and confirmed theme of Spring if Members so desire to bring something along those lines, mentioned charity donation MOCs to Brick Rodeo 2023 from the Group briefly, confirmed attendance at San Antonio Public Library's Pop Culture Convention in May, discussed the possibility of an event to commemorate the 10th Anniversary of the opening of our North Star Mall LEGO® Store, briefly discussed the current display and next display at Krystle's Brick Shop and discussed other LAN topics that might be of interest to the Group, mainly for this Meeting, the date of Project Support. Everyone enjoyed hearing about and seeing Members latest creations and builds.

## Attendance

AFOLs 15

## What We Enjoyed Most

Our virtual Community Meeting continues to fill the void of seeing each other personally and keeping the Group connected. Virtually allows Members to save gas, a very real thing now and save time by having a Community Meeting online and still accomplish subjects of interest to the Group. One good thing is that Members enjoy the chance to propose, plan and participate in TexLUG-SA Social gathering, to show off builds physically, do Parts Draft of small to medium sets and conduct builds with prizes. Additionally, each month, the Ambassador relays pertinent information from the LAN and gives Members an opportunity to discuss and comment if they desire. Members are encouraging each other to attend and actively participate in the virtual Community Meeting since we continue to put physical Community Meetings on hold, participation continues to be strong at virtual Community Meetings. Members continue to show the rest of the Group their LEGO® builds while working from home or checking into their workplace physically from time to time via these online Skype virtual Community Meetings.
