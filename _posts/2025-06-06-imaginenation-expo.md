# Event Summary

## Location

New Braunfels Convention & Visitors Bureau, 390 Seguin Ave, New Braunfels, TX 78130

## Summary

10 Members of our Group participated in the ImagineNation Expo for Kids at the New Braunfels Civic/Convention Center across three Show days and one setup day on Thursday. We had lots of our folks engaged with Show participants. Attendance was steady for the show and folks showed up to see the LEGO® themed display with running LEGO® trains. The folks asked lots of LEGO® based questions plus made comments on some of our more unique LEGO® creations on display. The most spoken comment was, "LEGO® has trains!". A LEGO® Lone Ranger (circa 2017) themed display was present on the end table made up of 4 baseplates by 4 baseplates with running Constitution Train from the theme, as well as a MOC depicting Prairie Dogs popping up and down in the lowlands of the display area. During the show days, visitors were asked to find the "easter eggs" LEGO® minifigures that were placed throughout the display. Parents and kids were excited to find the hidden treasures! The display contained several LEGO® Modular Buildings, MOC Modulars Style Buildings and several other LEGO® themed MOCs. And last but not least, are the LEGO® Trains that run all around our LEGO® City display located on multiple tables within the entire exhibit area of 20 feet x 25 feet. Thanks to all that contributed their LEGO® Brick MOCs, Sets, Trains and Vehicles that made the display possible for the long weekend! Setup for the Event is on Thursday afternoon for roughly 5 hours, and the Event runs 3 days, 7 hours each day.
