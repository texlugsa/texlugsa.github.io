# Event Summary

## Location

Krystle's Brick Shop at Rolling Oaks Mall, San Antonio, Texas 78247

## Summary

The LUG members enjoyed an opportunity to mix some of the LEGO® NINJAGO sets, Chinese New Year sets and Robert C's creation (Asian Gardens Palace) into a spring festival scene. The public enjoyed the use of sets and original creations to provide a spring festival with dragons, lions, and lots of blossoming trees and flowers.
