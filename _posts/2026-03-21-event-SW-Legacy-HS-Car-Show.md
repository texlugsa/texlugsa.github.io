# Event Summary

## Location

## Summary

The Community Members attending the display event enjoyed assembling the 2 table area in the lobby of the Southwest Legacy High School located near the only restrooms available for ALL attendees to use during the nearly 7-hour show. Our unintended assignment was to make sure the participants knew where the restrooms were located once in the lobby. The number of visitors increased as the day progressed and almost everyone stopped to look at our LEGO® Car Show. In between the visitors, Members discussed upcoming events like the Spring Train Show in New Braunfels and the new display at a public library in May, additionally, a relatively new Member shared her work experiences. Unexpectedly, the organizer of the event presented a small plaque to our Ambassador, thanking the Group for our support throughout the day.
