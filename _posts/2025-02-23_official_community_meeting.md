# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its second virtual Community Meeting (CM) of 2025 on 23 February 2025. 16 Group Members attended our virtual CM via Skype from their homes. Attendance at the virtual session was awesome! Topics discussed were from the LAN: nothing significant from the LAN to share, display at Krystle's Brick Shop created and looks awesome!, briefly discussed the Star Wars display at local Bricks and Minifigs - Bulverde, looking for creations, the New Braunfels Railroad Museum hosted Spring Train Show is 12-13 April 2025, the Group discussed Female AFOLs in the LUG and potentially how to get them more involved in the events and activities and Brickfest Live has moved its San Antonio show to September 2025. Favorite part of the virtual Community Meeting is the sharing of the latest creations and builds as we go "Around the LUG" near the end of the Community Meeting.

## Attendance

AFOLs: 16

## What We Enjoyed Most

Members enjoyed getting together and seeing everyone, even though it is virtual, since not everyone can do all the events or activities to see each other in person. Members enjoyed sharing their latest builds from their collections, what has been happening in their lives and their personal LEGO® themed interests, as we went "Around the LUG!"
