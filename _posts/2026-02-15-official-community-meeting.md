# Meeting Summary

## Location

[Krystle's Brick Shop in Rolling Oaks Mall](https://krystlesbrickshop.com)

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its second in-person Community Meeting (CM) of 2026 on 15 February 2026, another out-of-cycle meeting due to upcoming travel of the Ambassador. 11 Group Members attended our Community Meeting at Krystle's Brick Shop in the Rolling Oaks Mall. Topics discussed were several posts from the LAN about a LEGO Design Intern, On-line participation of a LEGO Employee at virtual Community Meetings and BETA Testers for the LAN; the Ambassador reaffirmed the process to recommend new Members for the Group due to continuing spam attempts on our Google Group Alias; next month's High School sponsored Car Show is on 21 March from 0800 - 1500, it looks like 7 Members will be contributing cars, trucks and minifigures to the show; the Spring Train Show at the New Braunfels Civic Center in April with more detail to follow next month. Additionally, the public library in Schertz confirmed it is interested in partnering with us to share LEGO creations and building techniques in the near future. The President of Brick Rodeo updated the Group with the latest planning for the convention later this year and stated that there are plenty of volunteer openings needed to support the event. The San Antonio LUG has volunteered to build the trophies for the upcoming convention and a date to accomplish those builds is in the works. The Group welcomed a potential new Member to the Community Meeting who recently retired and served in the military.

## Attendance

AFOLs: 11

## What We Enjoyed Most

Our favorite part of the Community Meeting for this month was another surprise random build with loose bricks from Krystle's Brick Shop brick tables, plus the sharing of the latest purchases, creations and builds as we go "Around the LUG" near the end of the Community Meeting. They enjoyed sharing their creations built from LEGO® bricks! An added bonus was welcoming a potential new Member and having them tell us about themselves and recent builds with LEGO® bricks.
