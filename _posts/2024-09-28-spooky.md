# Event Summary

## Location

Krystle's Brick Shop at Rolling Oaks Mall, San Antonio, Texas 78247

## Summary

The most enjoyable part of the gathering to create the display was that there was a LEGO® brick built monster coming out of the lake near the center. Additionally, it was wonderful to see several Group members hanging out to arrange their contributions into the display. It was nice to see a variety of diversity of the LEGO® sets that Members decided to contribute to the display. Overall, a good time was had by all that participated during the morning before the Store opened.
