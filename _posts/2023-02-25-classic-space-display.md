# Event Summary

## Location

Krystle's Brick Shop at Rolling Oaks Mall, San Antonio, Texas 78247

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) collaborated on a LEGO® Classic Space Display created by 5 Members contributing various neo-classic space MOCs and LEGO® Classic Space Themed Sets arranged in one of the windows of Krystle's Brick Shop located at Rolling Oaks Mall in San Antonio, Texas. The display contained a running LEGO® Monorail from the original Classic Space theme. The latest version of the Galaxy Explorer (10947) was suspended with fishing line over the display. The opportunity presented by the owners of Krystle's Brick Shop to give our Group a place to display LEGO® Classic Space themed based creations in their retail store. Several Members stopped by during the display period to review and take pictures of the MOC.
