# Event Summary

## Location

Krystle's Brick Shop at Rolling Oaks Mall, San Antonio, Texas 78247

## Summary

Each and every colleague enjoyed contributing their creation to the winter display. We learned from the Store Owner that all the public visitors to her Store loved the display; they wanted to buy it once they viewed the creations in the window over the Holiday period. The Winter Celebration gave a lot of folks inspiration to make their own similar creation next Winter. Several customers brought in some of their creations to show Store employees. One customer asked, “How much trade do I need to bring in to get that display in the window?" Of course, the Store Owner had to reply, "The display is not for sale, display only." Truly inspirational to our AFOLs that contributed to the window display.
