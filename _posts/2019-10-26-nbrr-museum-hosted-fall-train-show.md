# Event Summary

## Location

New Braunfels Convention & Visitors Bureau, 390 Seguin Ave, New Braunfels, TX 78130

[NBRR](https://newbraunfelsrailroadmuseum.org/nbrr/train-shows/)

## Summary

The Texas Brick Railroad (TBRR) LUG had 10 Members participate in the Event exhibiting in an 18 feet x 24 feet space a very detailed LEGO based display with several moving trains around Halloween themed areas. Since the Event in 2 days the hours reflected are for the first day (7 hours) on Saturday and second day (6 hours) on Sunday.
