# Event Summary

## Location

Bricks and Minifigs - San Antonio NE, 21850 Bulverde Road, Ste 102, San Antonio, TX 78259

## Summary

Most people were drawn to the display because it was Harry Potter. The most frequently asked question about the display was, "Where can the battle between Harry and Voldemort be found in the display?". The display seemed to be a success for the new venue, as this was the first display here.
