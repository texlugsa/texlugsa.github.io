# Meeting Summary

## Location

North Star Mall

## Summary

26/01/2020 Members gathered at North Star Mall to review the TexLUG-SA Calendar of Events for the coming months. The Group has registered to participate in the upcoming BiblioTechWest Library Makers Expo 2020 on 29 February and San Antonio Public Library Maker Faire 2020 on 21 March, details of these two events were discussed at the meeting. Members are getting excited about the upcoming NBRR Museum sponsored Spring Train Show in April 2020, it will be the largest event of the year. Additionally, Members assisted in answering questions poised by the LAN Mandatory Community Activity Survey. A few Members brought bricks to swap or give away to other Members.
