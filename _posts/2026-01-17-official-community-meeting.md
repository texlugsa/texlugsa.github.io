# Meeting Summary

## Location

[Krystle's Brick Shop in Rolling Oaks Mall](https://krystlesbrickshop.com)

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its first in-person Community Meeting (CM) of 2026 on 17 January 2026, another out of cycle meeting due to upcoming travel of the Ambassador. 16 Group Members attended our Community Meeting at Krystle's Brick Shop in the Rolling Oaks Mall. Topics discussed were the wrap up of the display at Bricks and Minifigs - Bulverde and short discussion that next display opportunity would be in August as Store Employees are using the space to prepare their MOC for Brick Rodeo 2026; reaffirmed the process to recommend new Members for the Group due to continuing spam attempts on our Google Group Alias; wrapped up the Winter Celebration display at Krystle's Brick Shop and placed the new Spring Celebration display at the start of the meeting, trying new format to encourage greater participation; firm date and time was discussed for the upcoming Car Show at local high school next month; confirmed that the Spring Train Show at the New Braunfels Civic Center in April is a go and finally resent Pick A Brick (PAB) survey link just in case Members felt a need to provide input as requested, previously posted to our Discord for initial comments. Additionally, the public library in Schertz is interested in partnering for us to share LEGO creations and building techniques in the near future. The President of Brick Rodeo shared the latest planning for the convention later this year and a new partnership with Kawacon Anime & Gaming Convention. Further the San Antonio LUG has volunteered to build the trophies for the upcoming convention.

## Attendance

AFOLs: 16

## What We Enjoyed Most

Our favorite part of the Community Meeting for this month was seeing everyone for the first time on the new year and gathering to prepare a new display for Krystle's Brick Shop window showing off creations arranged for the Spring Asian Celebration! Members shared their new LEGO® sets received for the Holidays as presents and how they were looking forward to building the new sets as we went "Around the LUG" near the end of the Community Meeting. Everyone enjoyed sharing about their year and future creations to be built from LEGO® bricks!
