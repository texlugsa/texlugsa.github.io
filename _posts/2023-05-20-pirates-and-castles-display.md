# Event Summary

## Location

Krystle's Brick Shop at Rolling Oaks Mall, San Antonio, Texas 78247

## Summary

Folks liked collaborating, coming together with their LEGO® My Own Creations (MOCs), building a larger display piece/MOC that was seen by hundreds of Mall walkers or Store visitors as they passed by the front window. It was fun not only building the LEGO® MOCs but figuring out how to place them as to then create something much grander … to create a story to be enjoyed by young and old alike.
