# Meeting Summary

## Location

[Krystle's Brick Shop in Rolling Oaks Mall](https://krystlesbrickshop.com)

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its second in-person Community Meeting (CM) of 2025 on 31 May 2025. 19 Group Members attended our in-person Community Meeting at Krystle's Brick Shop in Rolling Oaks Mall. Topics discussed were the preparation for setup and participation in the new ImagineNation Expo for Kids display at the New Braunfels Civic/Convention Center from 5 - 8 June 2025, potential displays at local libraries over the coming months and the future New Braunfels Railroad Museum hosted Fall Train Show on 25-26 October 2025. Our favorite part of the Community Meeting is the sharing of the latest creations and builds as we go "Around the LUG" near the end of the Community Meeting.

## Attendance

AFOLs: 19

## What We Enjoyed Most

Members gathered, a tremendous number, enjoyed getting together and seeing everyone! We also welcomed 3 new Members to the Group. After discussing a few house keeping items and upcoming events, the Member presence shared their latest creations, one Member brought her MOC of a Lord of the Ring set and the new Members thanked everyone for welcoming them with open arms. They are looking forward to future events and Community Meetings. Members of the Group after the meeting ended then shopped together at Krystle's looking through the tables containing LEGO® brick elements and other items on sale throughout the Shop.
