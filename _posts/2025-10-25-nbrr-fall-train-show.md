# Event Summary

## Location

New Braunfels Convention & Visitors Bureau, 390 Seguin Ave, New Braunfels, TX 78130

## Summary

12 Members of our Group participated in the New Braunfels Museum hosted Fall Train Show across two Show days and one setup day on Friday. Several Members engaged with Show participants during the weekend. Attendance was low for the Fall Train Show due to other (3) events on the same weekend in the City. The visitors asked lots of LEGO® based questions plus made comments on some of our more unique LEGO® creations on display. A LEGO® Medieval themed display complete with Castle was present on one end table that covered the entire space. A LEGO® Harry Potter themed village appeared magically surrounded by freight train carrying valuable wizard supplies. A MOC displaying a bunch of flowers and trees graced the City streets, bringing calm and tranquility to a normally busy City. Just down the lane, a spooky Castle and other haunted buildings and areas graced the final display area. The straight connector areas of the display contained several LEGO® Modular Buildings, MOC Modulars Style Buildings and several other LEGO® themed MOCs while traffic was steadily flowing around the streets. And last but not least, are the LEGO® Trains that run all around our LEGO® City display located on multiple tables within the entire exhibit area of 20 feet x 20 feet. Thanks to all that contributed their LEGO® Brick MOCs, Sets, Trains and Vehicles that made the display possible for the weekend! Setup for the Event is on Friday afternoon for roughly 6 hours, and the Event runs 2 days, 7 hours on Saturday and 6 hours on Sunday.
