# Event Summary

## Location

Bricks and Minifigs - San Antonio NE, 21850 Bulverde Road, Ste 102, San Antonio, TX 78259

## Summary

Interacting with the patrons of the Bricks and Minifigs - Bulverde store during both the setup and tear down of the display. Curious patrons stood watching the creation right on site of the display and asked questions while the display was being deconstructed and returned to individual Members that participated. Interaction with the public helps to promote the LEGO® Brand around the greater San Antonio area. Additionally, patrons were able to see firsthand how LEGO® creations (MOCs) and Sets can come together from LEGO® products and the versatility that can be achieved through creative building.
