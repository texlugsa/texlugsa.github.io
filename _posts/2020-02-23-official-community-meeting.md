# Meeting Summary

## Location

North Star Mall

## Summary

23/02/2020 - Members gathered at North Star Mall to review the TexLUG-SA Calendar of Events for the coming months. Later this week, Members will participate with their brick creations at the BiblioTechWest Library Makers Expo 2020 on 29 February. 10 Members in the Group enjoyed the fevered competition of a Small Speed Build using the LEGO® Set 60224, Satellite Service Mission that was provided as part of our Activity Support 2019. The eventual winner completed the build in 5 minutes and 51 seconds. Before the competition, the Members present read an article about AFOLs that was posted in the "The Patriot-News", (newspaper serving the Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, metropolitan area) Thursday, January 23, 2020. The Meeting wrapped up with “around the table” with all things LEGO®!
