# Event Summary

## Location

LEGOLAND Discovery Center - San Antonio

## Summary

Several Members setup and manned the MOC display table during the Adult Night at the LEGOLAND Discovery Center San Antonio downtown on the Riverwalk. The location of the table provided optimum viewing as adults from around the area attended the event. Based on comments our Group plans to bring more MOCs to next year's event to share with the Public. A good time was had by all, sharing, answering questions and discussing the MOCs on display.
