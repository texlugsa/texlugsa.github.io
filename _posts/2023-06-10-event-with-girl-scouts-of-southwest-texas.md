# Event Summary

## Summary

There was a lot of excitement and astonishment from both the parents, Girl Scouts, and siblings when visiting the room where the TexLUG-SA Members had setup up their LEGO® MOCs and Modulars with Patriotic Theme, despite the room being a bit hidden from other events going on as well throughout the building. A father that stayed behind while the rest of the family moved on to other ongoing events in the building enjoyed a deeper conversation about the LEGO® displayed with one of our Members. Some of the Girl Scouts, families, and employees looked forward to other collaborations with TexLUG-SA as far as additional displays for future open house days or future Girl Scouts events.

## Build Theme

LEGO® MOCs and Modulars with Patriotic Theme
