# Event Summary

## Location

Krystle's Brick Shop at Rolling Oaks Mall, San Antonio, Texas 78247

## Summary

For those that participated in the display, it was a great opportunity to put different eras of the LEGO® Pirates theme together into a fun scene. It was fun adding new touches to MOCs we have displayed before. The addition of gems and studs to create a sparkling water effect added a nice touch to the waterways of the display. The addition of "Easter Eggs" throughout the display is always fun especially when you get to see visitors’ reactions as they discover them. Whether it's a hidden dinosaur or LEGO® Minifigure of Jack Sparrow, everyone enjoys finding something unexpected in the display.
