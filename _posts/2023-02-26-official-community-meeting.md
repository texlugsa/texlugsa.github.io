# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its second virtual Community Meeting of 2023 on 26 February 2023. 15 Group Members participated virtually via Skype from their homes. The Group discussed a variety of topics, the tear down of the Winter Village Holiday display and setup of the Classic Space display at Krystle's Brick Shop, briefly discussed the SAMSAT Initiative to setup initial requirements gathering session with a few Group Members, discussed other LAN topics that might be of interest to the Group, new system for registering Members of a RLUG was discussed, details of upcoming events planned for March 2023 with the Girl Scouts of Southwest Texas and SWISD Medio Creek Elementary were firmed up. Everyone enjoyed hearing about and seeing Members latest creations including a new young Member and his Father who joined the meeting.

## Attendance

AFOLs 15

## What We Enjoyed Most

Our Virtual Community Meeting continues to fill the void of seeing each other personally and keeping the Group connected. Virtually allows Members to save gas, a very real thing now and save time by having a Community Meeting online and still accomplish subjects of interest to the Group. One good thing is that Members enjoy the chance to propose, plan and participate in TexLUG-SA Social gathering, to show off builds physically, do Parts Draft of small to medium sets and conduct builds with prizes. Additionally, each month, the Ambassador relays pertinent information from the LAN and gives Members an opportunity to discuss and comment if they so desire. Members are encouraging each other to attend and actively participate at the virtual community meetings since we continue to put physical community meetings on hold, and participation continues to be strong at virtual community meetings. Members continue to show the rest of the Group their LEGO® builds while working from home or checking into their workplace physically from time to time via these online Skype Virtual Community Meetings.
