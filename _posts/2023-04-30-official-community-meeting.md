# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its fourth virtual Community Meeting of 2023 on 30 April 2023. 9 Group Members participated virtually via Skype from their homes as the Ambassador conducted the meeting from the road on his way back from a week-long cruise. The Group discussed a variety of topics: wrapped up the NBRR Museum Spring Train Show, discussed participation at the SAPL hosted Pop Madness™, confirmed charity donation MOC for Brick Rodeo 2023 from the Group as created, built and donated by Robert C, discussed the possibility of an event to commemorate the 10th Anniversary of the opening of our North Star Mall LEGO® Store, and discussed other LAN topics that might be of interest to the Group, mainly for this Meeting, nothing new to report. Everyone enjoyed hearing about and seeing Members latest creations and builds.

## Attendance

AFOLs 9

## What We Enjoyed Most

Our virtual Community Meeting continues to fill the void of seeing each other personally and keeping the Group connected. Virtually allows Members to save gas, a very real thing now and save time by having a Community Meeting online and still accomplish subjects of interest to the Group. One good thing is that Members enjoy the chance to propose, plan and participate in quarterly TexLUG-SA Social gathering, to show off builds physically, do parts draft of small to medium sets and conduct builds with prizes. Additionally, each month, the Ambassador relays pertinent information from the LAN and gives Members an opportunity to discuss and comment if they so desire. Members are encouraging each other to attend and actively participate at the virtual Community Meeting since we continue to put physical Community Meetings on hold, participation was light this month. Members continue to show the rest of the Group their LEGO® builds via these online Skype virtual Community Meetings.
