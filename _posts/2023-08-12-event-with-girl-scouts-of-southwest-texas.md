# Event Summary

## Summary

The Girl Scouts of Southwest Texas Council, CEO, parents, and children truly enjoyed the opportunity for TexLUG-SA Members to come out and allow the kids to play with the LEGO® bricks, see the LEGO® City Stuntz sets on display, discuss possible future events with Girl Scouts, and converse with both parents and kids about LEGO® as a whole and joining the TexLUG-SA Group.
