# Event Summary

## Summary

2 Members of TexLUG-SA attended and displayed a Western Town Train Layout and Coral Reef My Own Creation (MOC). Thanks to the generous sponsorship of the Killeen Convention & Visitors Bureau, Bricks Killeen is a two-day exhibition of custom LEGO® creations and LEGO® merchandise. Several LEGO® User Groups from around the State of Texas attended while displaying creations made of LEGO® bricks and elements. The largest display of LEGO® model trains being set up by Texas Brick Railroad (TBRR). Several vendors were on hand to sell a variety of products.
