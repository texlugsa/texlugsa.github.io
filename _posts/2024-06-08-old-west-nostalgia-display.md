# Event Summary

## Location

Krystle's Brick Shop at Rolling Oaks Mall, San Antonio, Texas 78247 USA

## Summary

The most enjoyable part of this display was listening to the stories of the people that stopped by to visit the display. In particular, there was an older gentleman who loved the display so much he went home, used some old wood, scrap metal, and cloth to make his own wagon. He did his best to mimic the design and look of the LEGO® western wagon. One of the best parts of sharing our LEGO® creations is how it inspires others to be creative - either using LEGO® bricks or using other materials.
