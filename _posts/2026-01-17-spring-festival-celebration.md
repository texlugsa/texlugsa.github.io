# Event Summary

## Location

Krystle's Brick Shop at Rolling Oaks Mall, San Antonio, Texas 78247 USA

## Summary

This year’s display coincides with the mall’s Spring Festival celebrations, and TexLUG-SA Members have once again expanded on LEGO® themed sets to build a lively story of the season. From a colorful parade marching down the street to cheerful spectators enjoying a beautiful spring day, the scene captures the joy and renewal that come with a new spring. For the public, the display offers a window into how builders blend official LEGO® themed sets with their own creative designs. One of the most beloved features is the Asian‑inspired palace, surrounded by flowering trees and overflowing with vibrant blossoms. Visitors return year after year to see how the Group reimagines the festival, discovering new details while enjoying the traditions they’ve come to love. This annual display has become a cherished event for both the Group and the wider community, celebrating creativity, culture, and the shared excitement of spring.
