# Event Summary

## Location

New Braunfels Convention & Visitors Bureau, 390 Seguin Ave, New Braunfels, TX 78130

[NBRR](https://newbraunfelsrailroadmuseum.org/nbrr/train-shows/)

## Summary

Our Group had 10 Members participate in the first physical Event since the worldwide pandemic hit the USA in March of 2020. We built LEGO themed City plus Harry Potter "land" with running Trains around the entire exhibit in an 18 feet x 30 feet space. Members participating had ALL been vaccinated against COVID-19, this limited the number of Members that normally participate in the Event. We conducted a Spring Float challenge consisting of train themed entries including an engine decorated with flowers pulling the entries around the display. Setup for the Event is on Friday afternoon and the Event runs 2 days, 7 hours on Saturday and 6 hours on Sunday. The NBRR Museum planners were extremely pleased with the attendance at the Event.
