# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its tenth virtual Community Meeting of 2022 on 30 October 2022. 18 Group Members participated virtually via Skype from their homes. Attendance virtually was great this month. The Group discussed a variety of topics to include, latest subjects of interest from the LAN, a short discussion about the appearance on paper bags in LEGO® Sets, a brief update on the Group's Statue of Liberty Project, short discussion on replacing the current MOC displayed at Krystle's Brick Shop with a Winter Themed window display, wrap-up of successful NBRR Museum hosted Fall Train Show with theme of Fall and Halloween, attendance at the first TexLUG-SA Social held at a new Member's LEGO® Lair was a success, we agreed to hold our Holiday Party on 18 December 2022 plus TexLUG-Austin and TBRR announced that they were holding their annual Holiday Party on 11 December 2022. We briefly discussed in person meetings again, but the consensus is continuing with virtual meetings for now. Another new Member introduced himself and the rest of the Group did the same, sharing interests in which LEGO® themes they enjoyed. Everyone enjoyed hearing about and seeing Members latest creations and catching up as 2022 continues.

## Attendance

AFOLs 18

## What We Enjoyed Most

Our Virtual Community Meeting continues to fill the void of seeing each other personally and keeping the Group connected. Virtually allows Members to save gas, a very real thing now and save time by having a Community Meeting online and still accomplish subjects of interest to the Group. Members are encouraging each other to attend and actively participate at the virtual community meetings since we continue to put physical community meetings on hold, and participation continues to be strong at virtual community meetings. Members continue to show the rest of the Group their LEGO® builds while working from home or checking into their workplace physically from time to time via these online Skype Virtual Community Meetings.
