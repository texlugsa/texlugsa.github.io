# Event Summary

## Summary

TexLUG-SA Members enjoyed engaging with a variety of folks that attended the Pop Madness™ event during the hours dedicated by the library. The kids were fascinated by the variety of the LEGO® creations and sets that were on display. Each Member, when asked, described why they brought the LEGO® creations and sets to represent some faction of Pop Culture. In between the public stopping by our tables, Members got to get to know each other and develop an appreciation for each other. Additionally, Members toured other areas of the library to see other representation of Pop Culture that showed up for the day. Members were “treated” to a bit of excitement that did not end well for one library patron that did not like the answer given to him during his visit. All was well quickly.
