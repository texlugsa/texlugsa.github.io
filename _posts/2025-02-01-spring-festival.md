# Event Summary

## Location

Krystle's Brick Shop at Rolling Oaks Mall, San Antonio, Texas 78247

## Summary

For those that participated in the display, it was a great opportunity to blend several LEGO® themes together (Chinese New Year, Ninjago, and Monkie Kid) into a celebration of the spring festival. It was fun adding new touches to MOCs we have displayed before. The addition of a parade and a festival area next to the Asian Palace added new life to an old MOC. It also created opportunities for more members to participate in the display by making parade floats, festival activities, or new buildings. The addition of easter eggs throughout the display is always fun especially when you get to see visitors' reactions as they discover them. Whether it's a hidden ninja turtle or storm troopers marching in the parade, everyone enjoys finding something unexpected in the display.
