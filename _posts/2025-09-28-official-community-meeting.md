# Meeting Summary

## Location

[Krystle's Brick Shop in Rolling Oaks Mall](https://krystlesbrickshop.com)

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its fifth in-person Community Meeting (CM) of 2025 on 28 September 2025. 11 Group Members attended our in-person Community Meeting at Krystle's Brick Shop in Rolling Oaks Mall. Topics discussed were a synopsis of the LEGO® Rebuilding Communities (RLUG / RLOC) Session #2 and the presentation conducted by Jordan Paxton, AFOL Engagement, wrapped up Krystle's Brick Shop display, it looks gorgeous, well done Donny and Penny R and all the Members that contributed builds to promote and inspire the Public with spooky creations made from LEGO® bricks, a round of applause was given to Robert C for his Alamo creation and Workshop of the same name, sharing the build with all who attended, successful display was created at Bricks and Minifigures - Bulverde on Saturday, Members discussed, potential displays at local libraries over the coming months and the layout proposed for the next New Braunfels Railroad Museum hosted Fall Train Show was presented and discussed for the upcoming event on 25-26 October 2025 and reminded Members that Brick Rodeo 2026 All Access sales are open.

## Attendance

AFOLs: 11

## What We Enjoyed Most

Our favorite part of the Community Meeting for this month was the surprise random build with loose bricks from Krystle's brick tables, plus the sharing of the latest purchases, creations and builds as we go "Around the LUG" near the end of the Community Meeting. Members are excited for future meetings with the possibility of another creative build. Those Members participating in the New Braunfels Railroad Museum hosted Fall Train Show are excited to share their specially prepared creations to inspire the public to build with LEGO® bricks!
