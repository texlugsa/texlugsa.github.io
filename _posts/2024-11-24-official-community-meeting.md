# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its eleventh virtual Community Meeting (CM) of 2024 on 24 November 2024. 11 Group Members attended our virtual CM via Skype from their homes. Attendance was light as several Members were running errands preparing for Thanksgiving. Topics discussed were from the LAN: Townhall upcoming online: 10 December 2024, wrapped up another successful Train Show in New Braunfels with the least of amount of Members contributing to the display, completed new displays at our local LEGO® Resellers for the Holiday Season and potential Pop-Up Event at local restaurant next month. Briefly reminded Members about the TexLUG-SA Holiday Party next month on 14 December 2024. Our favorite part of the virtual Community Meeting is the sharing of the latest creations and builds as we go "Around the LUG" near the end of the Community Meeting.

## Attendance

AFOLs: 11

## What We Enjoyed Most

Members enjoyed getting together and seeing everyone, even though it is virtual, since not everyone can do all the events or activities to see each other in person. The regular Community Meetings let Members share or inquire about upcoming events or share recent finds about LEGO® products at local stores in the immediate area. Members also enjoyed sharing their latest creations and their personal LEGO® themed interests, as we went "Around the LUG!"
