# Event Summary

## Location

Freeman Coliseum and Expo Halls, 3201 E. Houston St., San Antonio, TX 78219

## Summary

The Members displaying most enjoyed the interaction with the public. It is always fun sharing with both children and adults how the creations are built and how they move/light up. The expression on people's faces when they see Anakin turn into Darth Vader or as the prairie dogs move is priceless. Life Under the Sea was also a big hit and it's fun for me when people find all the Easter eggs hidden in the creation. The Member that built the Prairie Dog MOC and Leviathan SEC did a great job of sharing how the motion in the Prairie Dog MOC and Avengers SEC worked. He also enjoyed explaining how the lenticular mosaic (Anakin to Darth Vader) worked using cheese slopes. Another Member spent most of his time showing people how his Modular sections were put together and our Lead Member shared how he created motion and lights in the Life Under the Sea MOC as well as the Spaceship MOC.
