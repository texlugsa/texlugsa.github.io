# Event Summary

## Summary

LBR Store at North Star Mall donated shopping bags filled with "goodies."

TexLUG-SA Members had the opportunity to teach girls about mosaic art with My Own Creations (MOCs) brought from their private LEGO® collections. This gave the Members the opportunity to assist Girl Scouts to use their creativity and imagination to make various LEGO® DOTS magnets, bracelets, patches, and sticker plates. Everyone, including the parents of the Girl Scouts enjoyed their time at this Event. The Girl Scouts Council asked the Group to participate in future events due to the success of this Event! The LEGO® Brand Retail (LBR) Store at North Star Mall donated small shopping bags filled with various stickers and other goodies.
