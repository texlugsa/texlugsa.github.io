# Event Summary

## Summary

6 TexLUG-SA Members were geared up and ready to participate in this Event, however, due to the Worldwide Corona-Virus Pandemic, the City of San Antonio, Texas closed ALL public libraries, as well as, a bunch of other public venues and the Event has been until 26 September 2020. Same Bat Time and Place. I already placed the postponed Event on the Community Calendar for later this year.
