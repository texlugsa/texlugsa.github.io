# Event Summary

## Location

## Summary

Thirteen Members of our Group participated in the New Braunfels Museum hosted Spring Train Show across two Show days and one setup day on Friday. Several Members engaged with Show participants during the weekend. The visitors asked lots of LEGO® based questions plus made comments on some of our more unique LEGO® creations on display. A LEGO® Monorail operated almost completely around the display. It was a hit with the kids that stopped by the display. A LEGO® Spring Market my own creation (MOC) sparked a bunch of comments about the variety of everything displayed within the market space. Lady Liberty arrived and folks that are regulars at the Train Shows were glad she was back. Several MOC buildings and the Bricklink Designer Program designed set Studgate Train Station made its debut in one of the corners of our horseshoe display. Several "Easter Egg Minifigures" were seen strolling about about outside the train station. The straight connector areas of the display contained several LEGO® Modular Buildings, MOC Modulars Style Buildings and several other LEGO® themed MOCs while traffic was steadily flowing around the streets. And last but not least, are the LEGO® Trains that run all around our LEGO® City display located on multiple tables within the entire exhibit area of 20 feet x 20 feet. Thanks to all that contributed their LEGO® Brick MOCs, Sets, Monorail, Trains and Vehicles that made the display possible for the weekend!
