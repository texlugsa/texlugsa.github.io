# Event Summary

## Location

Krystle's Brick Shop at Rolling Oaks Mall, San Antonio, Texas 78247 USA

## Summary

As a continuing collaboration with the local community, the display gives members of TexLUG-SA an opportunity to show off their building skills, work together, and learn from each other. The most enjoyable part of this display was creating a winter wonderland and seeing the amazement of people as they took in all the scenes and details hidden in the display.
