# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its fourth virtual Community Meeting of 2022 on 24 April 2022. 11 Group Members participated virtually via Skype from their homes. Attendance virtually is steady! The Group discussed a variety of topics to include, latest subjects of interest from the LAN, a short review of the subjects covered at the LAN Town Hall held on 7 April 2022, wrapped up support and attendance at the New Braunfels Railroad Museum hosted Train Show Jamboree from 9 - 10 April 2022 and initial discussion of a Group Project. We discussed in person meetings again, but the consensus is to continue with virtual meetings for now. We went around the Skype Session virtually and Members shared their latest LEGO® creations or builds over the past month and other general subjects associated with the love of the LEGO® brick within the Group. Everyone enjoyed hearing about and seeing Members latest creations and catching up as 2022 continues.

## Attendance

AFOLs 11

## What We Enjoyed Most

Our Virtual Community Meeting is filling the void of seeing each other personally and keeping the Group motivated during these trying times with the constant threats of new Omicron Variants of the COVID-19 virus that are affecting the local area, as well as other major places in the World. Masks are optional for customers, employees still wearing masks plus social distancing and limits of the number of folks (28) in the local LEGO® Store are still in effect. Members are encouraging each other to attend and actively participate in the virtual community meetings since we continue to put physical community meetings on hold, and participation continues to be strong at virtual community meetings. Members continue to show the rest of the Group their LEGO® builds while working from home or slowly returning to their workplaces physically via these online Skype Virtual Community Meetings.
