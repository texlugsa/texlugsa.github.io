# Meeting Summary

## Location

[Krystle's Brick Shop in Rolling Oaks Mall](https://krystlesbrickshop.com)

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its sixth in-person Community Meeting (CM) of 2025 on 2 November 2025, an out-of-cycle meeting due to upcoming travel of the Ambassador. 11 Group Members attended our Community Meeting at Krystle's Brick Shop in the Rolling Oaks Mall. Topics discussed were a very short report on the display at the LEGOLAND Discovery Center - was suddenly cancelled, wrapped up the Fall Train Show at the New Braunfels Civic Center in late October, an extension of the Krystle's Brick Shop display was documented, so builds to promote and inspire the Public with spooky creations made from LEGO® bricks continues for another week, a quick announcement of the TexLUG-SA Holiday Party and more information to follow, and Members discussed, potential displays at local libraries over the coming months plus reminded Members that Brick Rodeo 2026 All Access sales are open at discount until 1 December 2025. Finally, Members that wanted to build participated in the Flying Machine contest from the loose bricks on tables in Krystle's Brick Shop.

## Attendance

AFOLs: 11

## What We Enjoyed Most

Favorite part of the Community Meeting for this month was another surprise random build with loose bricks from Krystle's Brick Shop brick tables, plus the sharing of the latest purchases, creations and builds as we go "Around the LUG" near the end of the Community Meeting. Members that participated in the New Braunfels Railroad Museum hosted Fall Train Show shared their experiences that were unique, as the main Members that support the Train Shows were incapacitated or travelling. They enjoyed sharing their creations built from LEGO® bricks!
