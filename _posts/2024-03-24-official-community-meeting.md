# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its third virtual Community Meeting of 2024 on 24 March 2024. 18 Group Members participated virtually via Skype from their homes. Topics discussed were the Spring LAN Town Hall plus Video of Spring LAN Town Hall, the setup of the Asian Palace display at Krystle's Brick Shop and interaction with customers as the display was being assembled, the setup of Pirates Invasion at Bricks and Minifigs – Bulverde plus interaction with Owner, Employees and customers during the setup, short explanation of the cancellation of the opportunity to display at the Medio Creek Middle School Car Show, final planning for the displays at BrickFest Live, upcoming display at the New Braunfels Railroad Museum Spring Train Show in middle of April and future planning for train show in May. Several Members completed the Licensed Merchandise Concept Ideas Survey. One Member expressed interest in creating a video for the Celebrating 25 Years of LEGO® Star Wars with the "25-Second Film Festival". The Group welcomed another enthusiastic fan of LEGO® to our LUG and each Member shared their love of the LEGO® Brick during the “Around the LUG” part of the virtual meeting.

## Attendance

AFOLs: 18

## What We Enjoyed Most

This monthly virtual Community Meeting achieves one of our goals to keep in touch and plan events and activities soon and stay connected. Another goal continued to be fulfilled through attendance by LEGO® enthusiastic fans in the area seeking to join the Group. So this virtual meeting paid off this month as a potential new member joined our Group during a live meeting session and expressed an interest in joining regularly. During the “Around the LUG” portion, Members showed off their LEGO® builds and creations, sometimes it is just that they plan to build a recently acquired set.
