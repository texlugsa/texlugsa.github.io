# Event Summary

## Location

Medio Creek Elementary, 8911 Excellence Dr, San Antonio, TX 78252

## Summary

TexLUG-SA Members enjoyed sharing their LEGO® Speed Champion, Technic, City and Disney Cars® themed vehicles arranged in their own "Car Show" to complement the large fancy cars on display out in the parking lot of the elementary school. Members on hand interacted throughout the day with the public, teachers, school volunteers and especially with the young visitors to the display. The best news was that proceeds from the event will benefit the BOSS program at Medio Creek Elementary.
