# Event Summary

## Location

Bricks and Minifigs - San Antonio NE, 21850 Bulverde Road, Ste 102, San Antonio, TX 78259

## Summary

On various visits to Bricks and Minifigs - Bulverde after the placement of the Pirates Invasion Display, the lead Member heard incoming customers making various compliments about the LUG's display. One family really liked the display and how elaborate the layout looked with all the sets integrated together for one scene. Another customer commented "That they really appreciate the LUG for creating the displays for the business."
