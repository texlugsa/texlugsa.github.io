# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its ninth virtual Community Meeting of 2021 on 26 December 2021. 11 Group Members participated virtually via Skype from their homes. Attendance was great and wonderful considering it was the day after Christmas. The Group discussed and variety of topics to include, latest topics from the LAN, Ambassador answering the Annual LAN Survey and RLUG Report 2021, wrapping up the TexLUG Holiday Party, and surprising arrival of Play Bricks from Brick Rodeo 2021 (5,000 2x4 Bricks in Medium Azure) New Braunfels Fall Train Show in person Event. The Group welcomed a new Member who is moving from the Tampa Bay, Florida area to San Antonio, Texas in the Summer of 2022, Charlie I. Everyone looks forward to meeting him personally when we begin to meet in person sometime in 2022. We went around the Skype Session virtually and Members shared their LEGO® Christmas gifts, latest creations or builds over the past month and other general subjects associated with the love of the LEGO® brick within the Group. Everyone enjoyed hearing about and seeing Members gifts and latest creations.

## Attendance

AFOLs 11

## What We Enjoyed Most

Our Virtual Community Meeting is filling the void of seeing each other personally and keeping the Group motivated during these continued trying times with the surge of the new Omicron Variant of the COVID-19 virus and the continued effects of the Delta Variant of the COVID-19 virus. Masks, social distancing and limit of the number of folks in the local LEGO® Store are in effect, it is still recommended that individuals wear a mask when indoors throughout the greater San Antonio, Texas area. Members are encouraging each other to attend and actively participate at the virtual community meetings since we continue to put physical community meetings on hold, and participation continues to be strong at virtual community meetings. Members continue to show the rest of the Group their LEGO® builds while working from home via these online Skype Virtual Community Meetings.
