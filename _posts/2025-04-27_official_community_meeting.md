# Meeting Summary

## Location

The Food Court in North Star Mall

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its first in-person Community Meeting (CM) of 2025 on 27 April 2025. 6 Group Members attended our in-person Community Meeting at North Star Mall in the Food Court. Topics discussed were: the wrap up of the successful New Braunfels Railroad Museum hosted Spring Train Show on 12-13 April 2025, everyone enjoyed sharing their LEGO creations with the public during the event, especially assisting the young fans of LEGO search for hidden "easter eggs" in the Western themed portion of the display; and we will be having a social gathering after the change out of the display at Krystle's Brick Shop on 10 May 2025. Our favorite part of the Community Meeting is the sharing of the latest creations and builds as we go "Around the LUG" near the end of the Community Meeting.

## Attendance

AFOLs: 6

## What We Enjoyed Most

Members gathered enjoyed getting together and seeing everyone, doing show and tell, and playing a small LEGO® themed curiosity question game, then going to the local LEGO® Brand Retail Store, being the first Group in line to participate in the Make and Take together, and then shopping together for each Members favorite LEGO® themed products throughout the Store.
