# Meeting Summary

## Location

Online via Skype Video Application

## Summary

The Texas LEGO® User Group - San Antonio (TexLUG-SA) held its second virtual Community Meeting of 2022 on 27 February 2022. 14 Group Members participated virtually via Skype from their homes. Attendance virtually continues to be great! The Group discussed a variety of topics to include, latest subjects of interest from the LAN, another revisit of local reseller of LEGO® - Krystal's Brick Shop was discussed again and briefly discussed the SAMRA annual Model Railroad Jamboree that was ongoing this weekend. There was a brief discussion about the meeting in public again for March, however, the consensus was to hold off until more data is available for ongoing virus cases in and around San Antonio. We went around the Skype Session virtually and Members shared their latest LEGO® creations or builds over the past month, and other general subjects associated with the love of the LEGO® brick within the Group. Everyone enjoyed hearing about and seeing Members latest creations and catching up as 2022 continues.

## Attendance

AFOLs 14

## What We Enjoyed Most

Our Virtual Community Meeting is filling the void of seeing each other personally and keeping the Group motivated during these continued trying times with the new Omicron Variant of the COVID-19 virus in the local area. Masks, social distancing and limit of the number of folks in the local LEGO® Store are in still in effect, it is still recommended that individuals wear a mask when indoors throughout the greater San Antonio, Texas area. Members are encouraging each other to attend and actively participate in the virtual community meetings since we continue to put physical community meetings on hold, participation continues to be strong at virtual community meetings. Members continue to show the rest of the Group their LEGO® builds while working from home via these online Skype Virtual Community Meetings.
