**Texas LEGO User Group \- San Antonio**

**Code of Conduct**

Texas LEGO User Group \- San Antonio (TexLUG‑SA) is committed to maintaining a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment across all in‑person events, virtual programs, and online communities. Every member, guest, and participant is expected to uphold these standards.

**Unacceptable Conduct**

TexLUG‑SA does not tolerate any form of bias, harassment, intimidation, or abusive behavior. This includes—but is not limited to:

Language, behavior, or materials that intimidate, attack, demean, or threaten an individual or their work

Abusive or hostile behavior, including name‑calling, taunting, or targeted harassment

Any conduct directed at an individual or group based on:

race, ethnicity, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, disability, immigration status, military status, or any other aspect of personal identity

**Enforcement and Appeals Process**

Upon receiving a report of a conduct violation, the LEGO® Community Ambassador or a designated Board Member may issue an immediate temporary suspension from club activities if necessary to ensure community safety.

A formal review of the incident shall be conducted by the full Board of Directors within fourteen (14) days. Ultimate disciplinary action—up to and including a formal warning, extended suspension, or full termination of membership—requires a majority vote of the Board to ensure a fair and consistent evaluation. A member facing disciplinary action has the right to present a written statement to the Board before a final vote is cast.

**Agreement to the Policy**

By joining TexLUG‑SA and participating in any TexLUG‑SA activity, members acknowledge and agree to follow this Code of Conduct. 

To report any harassment, contact any of the TexLUG-SA Board members and/or LEGO® Community Ambassador or email texlugsa@gmail.com.
